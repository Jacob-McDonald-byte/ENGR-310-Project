FAQ

Common Questions

What's the difference between Free and Pro?

The free tier is a full-featured training log — track your lifts, diet, and PRs at no cost, forever. Pro adds the AI layer: automatic coaching feedback after every workout, predicted PR goals calculated from your actual data, adaptive weekly programming, and a personalized roadmap to help you hit those goals.

Do I have to enter my data into a chatbot to get AI feedback?

No — and this is a key difference. You never type anything into a chat window. Log your session as normal and the AI reads it automatically. It already knows your full lift history, your diet, and your progression patterns. The coaching summary appears on its own, every time.

How does PR prediction work?

The AI tracks your progression rate for each lift and uses that data to forecast your future personal records. These become your training targets — the app then builds a roadmap of workouts, nutrition, and recovery guidance to help you reach them on schedule.

Do I need gym equipment?

No. Lean Lifter supports barbell training, dumbbells, machines, bodyweight, and hybrid setups. Programs adapt to whatever equipment you have available.

How is this different from other fitness apps?

Most apps give you a static program or a generic chatbot. Lean Lifter reads your actual logged data, predicts where you're headed, and rewrites your plan every week based on real performance — no manual prompting, no guesswork.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. We never sell or share your personal health data. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.

When does the app officially launch?

We're targeting a public launch later this year. Beta testers get access now — join the waitlist to be first in line.
