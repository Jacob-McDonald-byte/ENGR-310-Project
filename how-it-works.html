The Process

Simple to Use. Powerful Results.

Four steps from sign-up to personalized coaching that actually moves the needle.

Step 01

Set Up Your Profile

Tell Lean Lifter about yourself — your training history, current lifts, body stats, and goals. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned lifter, the AI builds a baseline profile tailored to exactly where you are right now.

Step 02

Log Your Lifts & Diet

After each session, log your sets, reps, and weights in seconds using our streamlined interface. Simultaneously, track your meals and macros with our smart food database. The more data you feed Lean Lifter, the smarter it gets.

Step 03

Receive AI-Powered Feedback

Within moments of logging, Lean Lifter's AI analyzes your session. It identifies strength imbalances, flags recovery concerns, highlights nutrition gaps, and delivers a clear, written coaching summary — like having a personal trainer review every workout.

Follow Your Adaptive Plan

Every week, your program updates automatically. Did you crush your squats but stall on bench? The AI rebalances. Traveling and can only hit a hotel gym? It adapts. Life changed your schedule? Just tell the app — it adjusts without losing your momentum.

FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need gym equipment?

No. Lean Lifter supports barbell training, dumbbells, machines, bodyweight, and hybrid setups. Programs adapt to whatever equipment you have available.

How is this different from other fitness apps?

Most apps give you a static program. Lean Lifter rewrites your plan every week based on what you actually did — your real performance, your real diet, your real schedule.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. We never sell or share your personal health data. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.

When does the app officially launch?

We're targeting a public launch later this year. Beta testers get access now — join the waitlist to be first in line.
